Have you add your plugin to DM's hot deployment list? Look here:
https://trac.deepamehta.de/wiki/PluginDevelopmentGuide#SetupforHot-Deployment

Once you've done this DM must be restarted. Now, after reloading the browser window, your type should appear in the Create menu.

From now on your plugin is automatically redeployed each time you compile it (mvn clean package).

BTW: to get a list of installed plugins type "lb" (list bundles) in the Gogo shell (running in the terminal where you started DM). Your plugin is supposed to appear there.
