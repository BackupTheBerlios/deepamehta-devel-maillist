The scrollbar postions in the propery panel (e.g. in "Description" fields) 
schould be preserved. That is, the scrollbar position doesn't change if a 
topic is deselected and selected again.

It would be sufficient if the scrollbar positions are preserved within 
one client session (not across sessions). Technically spoken, the 
scrollbar positions must not necessarily be made persistent at server 
side but could be handled solely by presentation logic at client side. 
Thus, the realization should not be too hard.

This would improve usability reasonably, in particular in relation to the 
new search mimic (clickable result lists in property panel) and new 
email feature (clickable recipient lists in property panel).
