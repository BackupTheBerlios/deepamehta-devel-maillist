# General info about DeepaMehta development:

DeepaMehta 2 has reached its end-of-lifecycle. The development of 
DeepaMehta 3 has started 2 months ago. DeepaMehta 3 will become a 
complete rewrite which combines the experiences of the recent years 
with todays technology. DeepaMehta 3 starts as a pure 
HTML(5)/JavaScript/AJAX Webapplication with Apache CouchDB and Lucene 
at the Backend.

DeepaMehta 3 development takes place at GitHub:
http://github.com/jri

DeepaMehta 3 discussion takes place at Google Groups:
http://groups.google.com/group/deepamehta3
