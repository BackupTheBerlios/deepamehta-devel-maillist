Hi Torsten,
I am sensing in your post a central misunderstanding of
Deepamehta's take of the notions "opening" and "documents"
that you are referring to in your points 3.1 open books,
2.2 passages in documents, and in particular, 2.1 opening
two topics at the same time.

The old desktop paradigm concept of "Opening" becomes obsolete
because when you click at a topic *it is already open* in
the detail pane and what you are doing is essentially
*gazing with your mouse* : click with the mouse on what
you want to look at in the right pane, and (once DM2's
immediacy is restored) it is *immediately* viewable.

That's what the panes are for that you are questioning in 4.2.

Another sticking to the old desktop paradign of documents,
is revealed by (5th part) "attaching words with note snippets"
("Wörter ... mit Notizzettelchen versorge")
DM topics *can be* snippets themselves. So you are right
in (6th part) that text needs to be broken down ("Text
herunterbrechen").

BTW I, for one, would have loved to import your paragraphs
into DeepaMehta. As a replacement, I transcribed only a
few labels into Cmap tools, see the attached sketchpad.
