Dear developers,

on April 24 the 1st DeepaMehta Development Meeting takes place at Technische Universität Berlin. The meeting is aimed at developers who are interested in developing with the DeepaMehta platform. Ideally, actual (plugin) development projects are established while the meeting.

Agenda:
 - Very short DeepaMehta introduction
 - What a plugin can do -- the DeepaMehta development framework
 - Working groups

Time and location:
 - April 24, 2013
 - 4 p.m. - 8 p.m.
 - TU Berlin, Hardenbergstraße 36a
 - Room ER-136

Background:  the educationZEN applications and web interfaces are developed on top of the DeepaMehta software platform. educationZEN is a blended learning project taking place at Technische Universität Berlin.
www.eduzen.tu-berlin.de
(german)
